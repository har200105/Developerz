# Developerz

An App to connect developers across the world,allow them to share their projects. Developers can vote or upvote other's projects.Developers can also follow,unfollow other developers,view the projects developed by them.They can also connect with them through the social media links in their profile which developers may have added through the edit profile page.Developers can also search for the projects and other developers.
